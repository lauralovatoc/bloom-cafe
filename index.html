Drinks' Selection

*You can choose if your coffee will be hot or iced

Affogato

A decadent Italian classic — the Affogato pairs a scoop of creamy vanilla ice cream with a hot shot of bold espresso poured over the top. The result? A heavenly contrast of hot and cold, bitter and sweet. Dessert and coffee in one irresistible treat.


You can add additional ice cream scoops

Strawberry Latte

A dreamy blend of strawberry cream and velvety milk, layered with a smooth shot of espresso . This latte is a refreshing twist that feels like a sip of spring in every cup.


Pick you milk: dairy, oat or almond

Matcha Latte

Earthy, energizing, and beautifully green — our Matcha Latte is made with premium Japanese matcha whisked to perfection and blended with milk. Naturally rich in antioxidants and softly sweet, it’s a calm boost in every sip.


Pick you milk: dairy, oat or almond

Coffee Latte

A timeless favorite, our Coffee Latte is a smooth and creamy blend of rich espresso and steamed milk, topped with a delicate layer of microfoam. Simple, comforting, and perfectly balanced — it’s the kind of cup that never goes out of style.


Pick you milk: dairy, oat or almond

Espresso

Small in size but big on character — our espresso is crafted from carefully selected beans to bring out bold, rich flavors. Whether you're starting your day or in need of a midday boost, this concentrated shot delivers a smooth and intense experience. It's the foundation of every great coffee


You can add aditional shots of espresso

Strawberry Matcha

A vibrant fusion of sweet and earthy — this layered drink combines juicy strawberry, premium matcha and milk. Visually stunning and refreshingly unique, it’s the perfect balance of fruity and green-tea goodness in every sip.


Pick you milk: dairy, oat or almond

Menu

All of our dishes are freshly prepared every day using high-quality ingredients.

Parma Focaccia


Fluffy focaccia stacked with layers of Parma ham, creamy ricotta and fresh arugula. A savory, Italian-inspired sandwich perfect for lunch or a snack.

Cheescake


A rich and creamy classic New York-style cheesecake topped with a vibrant strawberry sauce. Smooth, indulgent, and perfect for dessert lovers.

Avocado Toast


Rustic sourdough topped with smashed avocado, a sunny-side-up egg, and a sprinkle of chili flakes. Fresh, wholesome, and packed with flavor.

Blackberry Pastry


Flaky puff pastry filled with a tangy berry cream and crowned with juicy blackberries. A sweet and tart pastry that’s perfect with coffee or tea.

Full Breakfast


A satisfying breakfast plate featuring a golden waffle, crispy bacon, and a sunny-side-up egg. A comforting and filling way to start the day.

Cinnamon Roll


Soft, gooey cinnamon roll swirled with buttery cinnamon sugar and finished with a smooth vanilla glaze. Warm, sweet, and utterly irresistible.

Eggs & Ham Bagel


Toasted bagel filled with fluffy eggs, savory ham, melted cheese, and greens. A satisfying, protein-packed option for breakfast or brunch.

Pan Au Chocolat


A buttery, flaky pastry filled with rich dark chocolate. A French classic perfect for a sweet breakfast or indulgent treat.

Tomato Toast


Grilled sourdough topped with whipped ricotta, heirloom tomato slices, olive oil, and herbs. A fresh and flavorful toast that's light yet satisfying.

French Toast

Omelet

Croissant


A buttery, flaky French pastry with a crisp outer layer and soft, airy interior. Perfect on its own or paired with coffee.
